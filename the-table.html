Starters & Mezze

The small things that stopped conversations
The Humtziki Our invention. Homemade hummus blended with our house tzatziki. Creamy, garlicky, completely addictive. You can't unknow it once you've had it. Customers ate it with a spoon. We didn't judge.

Roasted Red Pepper & Basil Hummus Freshly ground chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, roasted red peppers spiced with basil and a whisper of mint — and some other secrets we throw in when no one's looking.
Tzatziki Strained Greek yogurt, squeezed-dry cucumber, dill, lemon, good olive oil, and exactly the right amount of garlic. Which is always more than you'd expect.
Melitzonosalata Roasted eggplant, garlic, olive oil, spices. Smoky, silky, addictive.
Taramousalata A spicy-hot cheese dip made with feta and awesome. Mild if you asked nicely.

Hand-Rolled Spanakopita One at a time, by hand, the way it's supposed to be. Other fillings available if you asked nicely.

Avgo Keftethes Egg-and-herb fritters. Everyone asked what was in them. We never fully told.
Fresh-Baked Pita Dough made from scratch on premises. Baked small for dinner parties, baked hot for everything else.
