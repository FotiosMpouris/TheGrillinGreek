Press & Reviews

The Boston Globe · August 18, 2012

In Natick, all you need for supper.

"At The Grillin' Greek, a tiny, mostly takeout establishment, a flair for irresistible combinations is in evidence all over the menu. White pizza with artichoke hearts and feta? A 'Greekadilla' that marries the gooey delight of a quesadilla with the classic zing of spinach-and-feta pie? Yes and yes. The salata Kalamata, Greek salad with extra olives and lemony stuffed grape leaves, is all you need for supper."

— Louise Kennedy, The Boston Globe

What people actually said.

★★★★★ Sep 8, 2010 · First to Review
"A brand new place — food is amazing! Salads are super fresh and dressing is homemade. I am so picky and hate brown lettuce and other nasty bits, but this was a winner. Pita is fresh baked, I had the lamb (because I am a vegetarian :-) and the steak melt down (with garlic and bacon). Wow! Foti is the owner, say Hi and introduce yourself. Parking is a bit tricky, but they deliver. I am already dreaming of my next visit — mmmm."

— Jeffrey B., Cochituate, MA

★★★★★ Oct 22, 2013
"Our office recently moved from Boston to Natick so we had to find a new place for our monthly lunch delivery. This place is amazing. This is the second time I ordered the falafel wrap and I think the homemade pita wrap is probably the most delicious thing I've ever eaten. The falafel is better than any I had in Boston. The guys rave about the pizza and the chef salad was so full of meat, I never saw one so loaded. The prices seem too low for all that food! Also, the staff is maybe the friendliest I've ever encountered, after calling just once before Foteos (sorry if I slaughtered your name!) nearly remembered by name 3 weeks later."

— Yelp reviewer

★★★★★ Mar 15, 2011
"Absolutely delicious! Very small building on the corner of a busy intersection but well worth the trouble of getting there. It's a family owned establishment, the people working there have always been nice to me. The pizza is really great, if you're in Natick this is the place to get pizza, much better than Natick Pizza. Their GreekADillas are also amazing and unique; Spinach and Feta, mmmmm. Their salads are fresh and the pita bread is homemade. I recently moved closer to Boston and I've mentioned more than once how much I miss Grillin' Greek."

— Yelp reviewer

★★★★★ Mar 8, 2013

The Lil' Bo Peep salad is great — a big greek salad topped with roasted lamb and red peppers (by far the best salad in the area!). My boyfriend has had the falafel and the beef gyro, both of which he has loved. We'll definitely be returning!"

★★★★★ Sep 21, 2010
