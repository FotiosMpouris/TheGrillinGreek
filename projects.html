What People Said

Natick took notice.

Word of mouth did most of the work. The people who found us told the people who hadn't. But the press didn't miss it either. Here's some of what was written and said about The Grillin Greek and the Olymbos Pizza that started it all.

The Boston Globe

Coverage of The Grillin Greek, Natick

The Boston Globe covered The Grillin Greek as part of its MetroWest dining coverage, highlighting the restaurant's fresh, scratch-made Greek food and the signature items that set it apart from other spots in the region. The Humtziki and the grilled Greek salads were specifically noted.

The Natick Community

MetroWest's Open Secret

The Grillin Greek built its reputation almost entirely through word of mouth from the Natick community and surrounding towns. Framingham, Wellesley, Wayland, and Sudbury regulars made regular drives to Natick specifically for the food. For years it was the kind of place locals were protective of — a spot you told your close friends about, not the whole internet.
