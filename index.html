What We Made

The Signatures

Everything made from scratch. No shortcuts. No freezer bags. This is what Natick kept coming back for.

🫙

The Humtziki

Our original invention — homemade hummus blended with our house tzatziki. Creamy, garlicky, completely addictive. You won't find this anywhere else.

🥗

Mom's Greek Dressing

A family recipe we never sold. Until maybe now. Herbaceous, bright, and the reason our salads were unlike anything else in MetroWest.

🥙

Greek Tzatziki

Thick. Cool. Made the right way — real Greek yogurt, fresh cucumber, dill, a little garlic. The benchmark everything else gets measured against.

🔥

Grilled Greek Salads

Fresh greens, house dressing, and your choice of grilled marinated chicken or thinly sliced seasoned lamb. Simple. Perfect. Impossible to forget.
